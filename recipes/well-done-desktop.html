Well-Done Desktop Recipe

Above are some examples of how your well-done desktop might look when ready for consumption!

Required Steps:

  1. Grab your nearest desktop (Alienware cooks best).
  2. Make sure to take out every fan (will speed up cooking process).
  3. Add two RTX 4090's and one i9-13900K, with the cheapest power supply you can find.
  4. To preheat to 120 degrees celcius, we can simply boot up Crysis with a 16k youtube video in the background for a quick stress test.
  5. After we have hit sufficient temperature, we can shove a couple paint cans in the desktop case.

Optional Steps:

  • Fire extinguisher (Extra flavour).
